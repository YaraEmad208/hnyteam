Pizza Hut

The story of Pizza Hut began in May 1958.
Dan and Frank Carney opened their pizza restaurant in Wichita,
Kansas and it was only 550 square meters. Dan and Frank and their friends Richard Beamer
and John Bender made the pizza themselves. "Everyone was intrigued and interested in the product,
Dan Carney recalled. "They were watching us enjoy the batter over our heads..."
Pizza Hut has always been a pioneering and innovative place where everyone shares their experiences and knowledge.
As Frank Carney recalls, "Pizza's strength was in the presence of so many innovative people."
